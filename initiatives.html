General Plan Update

The new General Plan that was recently approved with our advocacy zones for up to 57,000 new housing units in mixed-use, multifamily areas near transit.

Next

Safe Bike Infrastructure

Construction of the first protected bike lane in Irvine has started at Cadence after we pushed city council. But it's just a start. We envision off street trails and on street separated lanes everywhere.

Next

Irvine Connect Shuttles

We pushed to make Irvine's first city bus line run every 20 minutes instead of 30/60 peak/offpeak. Ridership has almost doubled since launch, and we're advocating for new bus lines to build a whole transit network.

Next

Orange County Maintenance Facility

Metrolink's SCORE program calls for regional trains connecting Irvine to the rest of OC and LA every 15 minutes by 2028. To do that, the agency needs a trainyard near Irvine Station. We are fighting to make sure the city approves it without further delay.

Next

Walnut Rail Line

Inspired by San Bernardino's Arrow rail line, the Walnut Line is a proposal to use existing rail right of ways to create a modern backbone train service in Irvine using cutting edge multiple-unit trains from Europe. We are working with local and state officials to see this realized.
